Understand Cars

ENGINE

A motor designed to convert one form of energy into a mechanical energy. It creates combustion to power your vehicle and make it move. 

TRANSMISSION

A machine that provides controlled application of power for your vehicle. It adapts the output of the internal combustion engine to drive the wheels. 

SUSPENSION

A system that allows relative motion between shock absorbers and spring through the vehicle. It supports the road holding, handling, and ride quality which are commonly at odds with each other. 

BRAKES

A mechanical device that inhibits motion by absorbing energy from a moving vehicle. It is the one responsible for slowing and stopping your vehicle by using friction. 

EXHAUST

A system that movies the burnt air-fuel mixture out of the engine. It is also responsible to clean this emission and reduce unnecessary noise generated. 

TIRES

A component that surrounds the wheel's rim to transfer a vehicle's load from the axle through the wheel to the ground and it provides the traction on the surface road.
